About Upload Magic

Upload Magic is an ACO brand started in 2020 due to COVID-19, this lockdown and time where everyone was at home made a need for a way for students to share files to teachers, and for staff to share documents for work. This FREE tool was made to help the world and make education and work easier for people, take a look at the demo version under and email support to order a custom magic page for your school, class, or company! We hope Upload Magic is able to make it easier for you and your team to get work done better! Thanks for checking us out and hope we are able to serve you!
